Why Respondent Pro?

The Problem: Respondent.io has poor filtering capabilities. I found myself spending a lot of time curating projects, hiding them, and occasionally I forgot about it (lost opportunities).

The Solution: That's why I built Respondent Pro to help manage all the projects and automate the filtering to maximize my profits.

RespondentPro is a hobby project I created for myself, but decided to share with the world. The first 1,000 projects hidden you'll get for free - you'll get all the features (AI-driven personalized curation of projects) at no cost.

After that, you can pay as you go at $5 for every 1,000 projects hidden, or get a lifetime membership at $50 for unlimited projects.
