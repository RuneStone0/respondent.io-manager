Respondent Pro

AI-powered project curation for Respondent.io

Maximize your profits by automating project filtering and never miss an opportunity

The Problem

Poor Filtering Capabilities: Respondent.io has limited filtering options, forcing you to manually curate projects one by one. This time-consuming process often leads to missed opportunities when you forget to check back regularly.

No Email Notifications: Respondent.io doesn't send email notifications when projects are ready for review. Without proactive alerts, you're likely to leave money on the floor by missing opportunities that you would have otherwise applied to.

Time Investment vs. ROI: Without visibility into hourly rates and proper filtering, you risk spending significant time on projects with low incentives—curating, completing screening surveys, and conducting interviews—only to realize a very low return on investment.

The Solution

Respondent Pro was built to help you manage all your projects and automate filtering to maximize your profits. Our AI-driven platform learns from your preferences and continuously improves to save you time and increase your earnings.

What Respondent Pro Gives You

Hourly Rate Filtering

Automate filtering based on hourly rates to focus your time where ROI matches your requirements. This critical metric isn't even visible in respondent.io.

Max Incentive Filtering

Focus on high-value projects. Avoid wasting time on low-incentive projects that result in poor ROI after all the screening and interview time.

Location-Based Filtering

Automate filtering based on location preferences—choose between remote and in-person projects that fit your lifestyle.

Topic-Based Filtering

Filter projects by topics that interest you, ensuring you only see opportunities aligned with your expertise and interests.

Customizable Reminders

Respondent.io doesn't provide reminders. RespondentPro lets you configure Daily or Weekly email reminders so you never miss an opportunity.

AI-Powered Learning

When you hide projects, we record why you made that decision and use that knowledge to automatically hide similar projects in the future, further reducing curation time.
